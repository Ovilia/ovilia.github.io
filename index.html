TAO

Being one of the most famous Chinese philosophers, Laozi inspired and enlightened people for thousands of years with Tao, the universal way the world runs in. His masterpiece, Tao Te Ching, was written unwillingly in around 6th century BC. Because he didn't want to talk too much about Tao himself. He who knows won't talk, he who talks don't know, as he put it.

In Taoism philosophy, everything is about relativity. It is ugliness that defines beauty as it is black that defines white. This does not only inspire me a lot when I learn sketching, but also direct me in everyday life.

I learned to take things easily as they come along and not to contrive too much, which doesn't necessarily mean being optimistic. On the contrary, I would do my best to see what can be achieved and accept it when it's time to let go.

Of course, Tao is a lot more than that. You have to make it out by yourself, as Laozi said, Tao that can be explained is not the unchanging Tao.

CODES

I'm glad that as a creative programmer, my enthusiasm lies in creating useful applications to make life easier.

Randocy is a project to find online movies with high score on Douban. I made this because one day in my summer vocation in 2012, I was boring finding movies on web sites like Youku and Tudou. The problem is that scores are not so accurate on those sites (a guess is that there are some business related reasons). So I made Randocy to sort using Douban scores.

As I said before, without the guidance of Tao, the programming skills are meaningless by themselves. It's much more important to dig out potential needs and present with user-friendly interfaces.
